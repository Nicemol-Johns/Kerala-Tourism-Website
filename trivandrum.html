Trivandrum

Trivandrum, also known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and is home to several famous landmarks, including the Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam Beach, and the Napier Museum. The city is also a major hub for technology and education in Kerala. Overall, Trivandrum is a vibrant city that offers a blend of tradition and modernity.

Magic Planet

Magic Planet is a popular amusement park known for its thrilling rides, entertainment shows, and family-friendly atmosphere. It is a great spot for fun-filled activities and a popular destination for families and groups of friends.

Vizhinjam Lighthouse

The Vizhinjam Lighthouse is a historic lighthouse known for its picturesque location, panoramic views of the sea and coastline, and the maritime history it reflects. It is a popular tourist destination and a great spot for photography and sightseeing.

Trivandrum Zoo

The Trivandrum Zoo is known for its diverse collection of animals, birds, and reptiles and its beautiful park-like setting. It is a great spot for families and nature lovers, and a popular destination for tourists visiting the city.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is a beautiful beach known for its scenic beauty, golden sand, and serene atmosphere. It is a popular tourist destination and a great spot for relaxation, swimming and water-based activities like surfing and sunbathing.

Agastya Mala

Agastya Mala is a mountain range located in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India, known for its diverse flora and fauna, rich biodiversity, and scenic beauty. It is also considered a sacred site in Hindu mythology.
