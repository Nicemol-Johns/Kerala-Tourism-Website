Alappuzha

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a beautiful town located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is renowned for its serene backwaters, sprawling paddy fields, and network of canals. Often referred to as the "Venice of the East," Alappuzha is a popular tourist destination and a hub for houseboat cruises. The town also has a rich history and is dotted with several ancient temples and churches, reflecting its diverse cultural heritage. With its scenic beauty and cultural charm, Alappuzha is a must-visit destination in Kerala.

Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its rich history and cultural heritage, with several important landmarks and sites, including the iconic Padmanabhaswamy Temple, which is one of the richest temples in the world. The city is also known for its beautiful beaches, including the popular Kovalam Beach, and the Vizhinjam International Deepwater Multipurpose Seaport, which is set to become a major hub for international trade in South India. The economy of Thiruvananthapuram is driven by industries such as information technology, tourism, and agriculture, with a focus on crops such as coconut, rubber, and spices. The city is also home to several research and development institutions, including the Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre and the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research.

Idukki

Idukki

Idukki is a mountainous district located in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, with lush green hills, dense forests, and scenic waterfalls. Idukki is home to several important wildlife sanctuaries, including the Periyar National Park, which is home to many endangered species such as the Bengal tiger and Indian elephant. The district is also famous for its hydroelectric power projects, including the Idukki Dam, which is the largest arch dam in Asia. The economy of Idukki is driven by agriculture, with a focus on crops such as tea, coffee, and spices. The district is also known for its flourishing tourism industry, with visitors attracted to its natural beauty and eco-tourism opportunities.
