Idukki

Idukki is a mountainous district located in the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its natural beauty, with numerous hills, waterfalls, and forests. The district is home to the famous Idukki Dam, which is one of the largest arch dams in Asia. The district is also home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, which is a popular tourist attraction. Overall, Idukki is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Hill View Park

The Idukki Hill View Park is a scenic park offering stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and the Idukki dam. It is a popular tourist destination and a great spot for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts.

Idukki Dam

The Idukki Dam is a double curvature arch dam located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India, built across the Periyar River. It is one of the largest arch dams in Asia and a major source of hydroelectric power for the region.

Wildlife Sanctuary

The Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area located in the forest regions of the Idukki district of Kerala, India, known for its diverse flora and fauna. It is a popular destination for nature lovers, bird watchers, and wildlife enthusiasts.

Vagamon

Vagamon is a hill station known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and tranquil atmosphere. It is a popular tourist destination and a great spot for adventure activities like trekking, paragliding, and rock climbing.

Nandukani

The Nandukani Viewpoint is a scenic spot located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India, offering panoramic views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and tea plantations. It is a popular tourist destination and a great spot for photography and nature lovers.
